🔎 Reading B2 — The Rise of Urban Farming Chờ duyệt (in_review)
Đoạn đọc hiểu đính kèm
"Urban farming has moved from rooftop hobby to serious business. Vertical farms stacked inside disused warehouses now supply salad greens to supermarkets within hours of harvest, cutting transport emissions and sharply reducing the need for chemical treatments…"

Câu hỏi: According to the passage, what is one key benefit of vertical farming?

💡
Giải thích: Đoạn cuối viết "sharply reducing the need for chemical treatments" — paraphrase của "greatly reduces the amount of pesticides needed". Các phương án A, C, D không được nhắc tới trong bài.
